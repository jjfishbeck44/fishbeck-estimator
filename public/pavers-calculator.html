How the Paver Calculator Works

Each paver covers length × width (in inches) ÷ 144 square feet. We add waste and divide your patio area by that to get the paver count, then size a 4" gravel base and 1" sand bed by volume:

pavers = ⌈ area × (1 + waste%) ÷ paver sq ft ⌉

Add plastic/metal edging around the perimeter and polymeric sand to lock the joints.
