How the Fence Calculator Works

We divide your fence length by the post spacing to get sections, then add one post to close the run. Rails multiply by sections, and concrete scales with posts:

posts = ⌈ length ÷ spacing ⌉ + 1

Add an extra post at each gate and corner, and check local setback rules and call 811 before you dig.
