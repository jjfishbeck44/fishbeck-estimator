    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate are these estimates?

    These are AI-generated ranges based on typical Twin Cities project costs. They give you a realistic ballpark — most final proposals land within the estimated range. For a binding price, request a formal proposal.

    How quickly can Fishbeck start my project?

    We typically turn around proposals the same day and can begin most projects within 1–2 weeks, depending on scope and materials availability.

    What areas do you serve?

    We serve the entire Twin Cities metro area — Minneapolis, St. Paul, and surrounding suburbs including Bloomington, Eagan, Richfield, Brooklyn Park, Maple Grove, and more.

    Do you work with property managers?

    Absolutely. Most of our clients are property managers with multi-unit buildings. We handle unit turns, common area maintenance, and large-scale renovations.

    What's not included in the estimate?

    The estimator covers our core services: unit turns, painting, flooring, drywall, kitchens, bathrooms, roofing, demolition, and exterior work. Specialized trades like HVAC, electrical panels, plumbing rough-in, and structural engineering are flagged as out-of-scope.
