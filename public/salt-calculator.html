How the Salt Calculator Works

We apply a coverage rate per 1,000 sq ft to your lot size, then multiply by the number of applications and divide by your bag size:

salt (lbs) = (area ÷ 1,000) × rate × applications

Over-salting wastes money and harms plants and concrete. Rock salt stops working below about 15°F — use a calcium- or magnesium-chloride blend in deep cold.
