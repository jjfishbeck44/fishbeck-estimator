How the Sod Calculator Works

We add a small waste factor to your area, then divide by the coverage of a roll (≈10 sq ft) or a pallet (≈450 sq ft) and round up:

rolls = ⌈ area × (1 + waste%) ÷ 10 ⌉

Order sod for delivery the day you install it — it’s perishable and should be laid within 24 hours.
