How the Mulch Calculator Works

Mulch is sold by volume. One cubic yard of mulch covers about 324 square feet at 1 inch deep, so we multiply your bed area by the depth (in inches) and divide by 324:

cubic yards = (area in sq ft × depth in inches) ÷ 324

For bagged mulch, we convert to loose cubic feet (area × depth ÷ 12) and divide by your bag size. We always round up so you don’t run short — it’s common to add about 5–10% extra for uneven beds and settling.
