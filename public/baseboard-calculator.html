How the Trim Calculator Works

We take the room perimeter (2 × (length + width)), subtract about 3 ft per doorway, add waste for miters, then divide by your stock length and round up:

pieces = ⌈ (perimeter − doors) × (1 + waste%) ÷ stock length ⌉

Buy a couple extra sticks — inside/outside corners and scarf joints use more than the raw length.
