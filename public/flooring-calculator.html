How the Flooring Calculator Works

We take your room area, add a waste percentage for cuts and defects, then divide by the coverage printed on the carton and round up to whole boxes:

boxes = ⌈ area × (1 + waste%) ÷ sq ft per box ⌉

Order all boxes from the same lot or dye-run for a consistent color, and keep a spare box for future repairs.
