How the Wallpaper Calculator Works

We compute wall area (perimeter × height), subtract doors (~21 sq ft) and windows (~15 sq ft), add pattern-match waste, then divide by the usable coverage of a roll:

rolls = ⌈ net area × (1 + waste%) ÷ usable per roll ⌉

Pattern repeats increase waste — buy all rolls from the same batch/run number for a seamless match.
