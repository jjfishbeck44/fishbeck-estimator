How the Drywall Calculator Works

For a room, wall area is the perimeter times the ceiling height: 2 × (length + width) × height, plus the ceiling (length × width) if you include it. We add your waste allowance, then divide by the sheet size and round up:

sheets = ⌈ area × (1 + waste%) ÷ sheet sq ft ⌉

Materials are rule-of-thumb estimates: about 32 screws per 4×8 sheet, roughly 1 gallon of joint compound per ~108 sq ft, and about 370 ft of tape per 1,000 sq ft. Round up to standard package sizes and keep a little extra on hand.
