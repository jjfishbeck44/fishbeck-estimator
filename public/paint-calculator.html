How the Paint Calculator Works

We start with your wall area — for a room, that’s the perimeter times the ceiling height: 2 × (length + width) × height. Then we subtract about 21 sq ft per door and 15 sq ft per window to get the area you’ll actually paint.

gallons = (paintable sq ft × coats) ÷ coverage per gallon

One gallon typically covers about 350 sq ft per coat on primed walls. We round up to whole gallons. New drywall, bold color changes, or rough textures soak up more, so consider a primer coat or a little extra.
