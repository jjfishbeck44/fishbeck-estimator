How It Works

Profit is your ARV minus everything you put in: purchase, rehab, holding costs (taxes, insurance, interest), and selling costs (commission, closing). ROI is profit divided by the cash you tie up. The 70% rule — offer no more than 70% of ARV minus rehab — is a quick sanity check, not a guarantee.
