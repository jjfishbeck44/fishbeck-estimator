How the Concrete Calculator Works

We turn your slab into a volume — area times thickness — add waste, then convert to cubic yards (27 cu ft per yard):

cubic yards = area × (thickness ÷ 12) ÷ 27

Ready-mix is sold by the yard (usually in 0.25-yard steps). Bag yields: 80 lb ≈ 0.60 cu ft, 60 lb ≈ 0.45, 40 lb ≈ 0.30. Order a little extra — you can’t add concrete to a pour that’s short.
