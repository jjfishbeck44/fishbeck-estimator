Subject: RFP 2026-S04 — Summer Classroom Repainting, Roosevelt Elementary
Agency: Saint Paul Public Schools (SPPS)
Posted: 2026-06-12
Due: 2026-07-01 16:00 CDT

--- Notification body ---

Saint Paul Public Schools invites proposals for the summer repainting of
22 classrooms, 4 hallways, and 2 common areas at Roosevelt Elementary.
Work window: 2026-07-20 through 2026-08-15. Estimated value
$28,000 – $42,000.

No performance bond required for contracts under $50,000.
Prevailing wage applies (school district policy).
DBE/MBE participation strongly encouraged but not required.

--- Spec excerpt ---

SCOPE OF WORK

1. Surface preparation
   - Patch nail holes and minor drywall damage with lightweight spackle
   - Sand patches smooth, prime as needed
   - Wipe down all surfaces

2. Paint
   - Walls: two coats interior latex, eggshell, "or equal" to Sherwin-Williams
     ProMar 200 or Benjamin Moore Ultra Spec 500. Color: existing palette
     (SPPS will provide swatch numbers; contractor may substitute equivalent
     manufacturer colors with district approval).
   - Trim and doors: semi-gloss, contractor's choice of manufacturer
   - Ceilings: flat white, contractor's choice

3. Cleanup and touch-up at substantial completion

EXCLUSIONS
- No HVAC, electrical, or plumbing work
- No flooring
- No window replacement

Contractor may use district-approved alternates with submission of cut-sheets
at any time during the project. District facilities team will review within
3 business days.
