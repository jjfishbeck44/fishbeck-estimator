Subject: IFB 2026-114 — Government Center Renovation Phase II
Agency: Dakota County
Posted: 2026-06-11
Due: 2026-07-22 14:00 CDT

--- Notification body ---

Dakota County is soliciting bids for Phase II renovation of the Dakota
County Government Center, Hastings campus. Scope includes mechanical
systems upgrade, partial interior refresh, and accessibility improvements.
Estimated value $1.2M – $1.8M.

Performance and payment bonds 100% required. AIA A201 general conditions.
Prevailing wage applies. Mandatory pre-bid meeting and site walk
2026-06-25 at 10:00 AM. Bidders must demonstrate $5M general aggregate
liability and prior experience on at least three projects of similar
size.

--- Spec excerpt ---

DIVISION 23 — HVAC
- Replace 3 existing rooftop units with 25-ton high-efficiency RTUs
- Replace VAV boxes (qty 42) and rebalance air distribution
- Replace 4,200 linear feet of supply ductwork
- New BAS controls integration with existing Honeywell platform

DIVISION 26 — ELECTRICAL
- Replace main switchgear (2000A service)
- New emergency generator (250 kW)
- LED retrofit throughout (qty 380 fixtures)

DIVISION 22 — PLUMBING
- Replace 14 ADA-compliant restroom fixtures
- New hot water recirculation system

DIVISION 09 — FINISHES (minor portion of total scope)
- Repaint affected ceilings and walls after mechanical work,
  contractor's choice of low-VOC interior latex
- Replace floor tile in 3 corridors with district-standard VCT
  (Armstrong Standard Excelon, color VC-149, or approved equal)
- Patch drywall as required from mechanical/electrical demolition

GENERAL CONDITIONS
- Single prime contract; subcontracting requires prior county approval
- Substantial completion required by 2026-12-15
