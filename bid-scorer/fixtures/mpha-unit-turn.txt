Agency: Minneapolis Public Housing Authority
Posted: 2026-06-10
Due: 2026-06-28 14:00 CDT

--- Notification body ---

The Minneapolis Public Housing Authority (MPHA) is soliciting bids from
qualified contractors for unit turnover services at 14 scattered-site
properties in North and South Minneapolis. Estimated contract value:
$38,000 – $52,000. Single-year contract with two one-year renewal options.

Mandatory pre-bid walk-through: 2026-06-17 at MPHA Field Office, 10:00 AM.

Performance and payment bond NOT required for contracts below $75,000.
Prevailing wage does NOT apply (under federal threshold).

--- Spec excerpt ---

SECTION 09 91 23 — INTERIOR PAINTING

2.1 PAINT MATERIALS
A. Interior latex paint, low-VOC, eggshell finish. Contractor's choice of
   manufacturer subject to MPHA acceptance of color match against existing
   palette samples retained at the Field Office.
B. Primer: tinted to match topcoat. Manufacturer's choice.
C. Doors and trim: semi-gloss latex enamel, contractor's choice.

SECTION 09 65 19 — RESILIENT TILE FLOORING

2.1 LUXURY VINYL PLANK
A. Provide LVP flooring meeting the following performance criteria:
   - Minimum 5mm total thickness, 20 mil wear layer
   - Glue-down or click-lock installation
   - Class A fire rating per ASTM E648
   Acceptable manufacturers include but are not limited to Shaw, Mohawk,
   Armstrong, or APPROVED EQUAL. Substitutions permitted upon submittal
   of manufacturer cut-sheets and physical samples at any time.

SECTION 06 40 23 — CABINET HARDWARE
A. Replace existing knobs and pulls. Brushed nickel finish. Manufacturer's
   choice. Field-verify hole spacing.

Out of scope: HVAC service, electrical panel work, plumbing rough-in.
Minor patching of drywall and trim included.
