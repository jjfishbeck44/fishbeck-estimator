## Intro
Fishbeck Innovations partners with dependable, licensed Minnesota trades on
renovations, rental turns, assessments, and repairs across the Twin Cities. If you
do quality work and communicate, we want a long-term relationship.

## Why partner with us
- Steady, repeat project flow across the metro
- Clear scopes and fast, reliable payment
- Professional project management — you focus on the work

## What we require
- Active **Minnesota license** for your trade (verified via MN DLI)
- Current **insurance** (general liability; workers' comp where applicable)
- W-9 on file
- A track record of quality and reliability

## Trades we work with
Electrical, plumbing, HVAC, drywall, paint, flooring, roofing, concrete, framing,
carpentry, demolition/haul, cleaning, and exterior.
