## Intro
Walk-through assessments that document a property's condition, flag issues, and
outline recommended work with cost ranges. Ideal before a purchase, a rehab, or
building a maintenance plan.

## What's included
- Condition walkthrough and documentation
- Issue identification and prioritization
- Recommended scope with cost ranges
- Clear report you can act on

## Who it's for
Homebuyers, investors evaluating deals, and property managers planning maintenance.

## Process
1. Schedule the walkthrough
2. On-site assessment
3. Documented report with cost ranges
4. Optional scope & estimate for the work
