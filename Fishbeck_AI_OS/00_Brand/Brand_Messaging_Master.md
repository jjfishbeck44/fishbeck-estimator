# Brand Messaging Master

The master reference for how Fishbeck Innovations talks about itself. Claude reads
this before writing any customer-facing content.

## One-liner

Fishbeck Innovations is a Twin Cities construction and property maintenance
company that gives homeowners, investors, and property managers fast, transparent
estimates and reliable work — start to finish.

## Positioning statement

For Twin Cities property owners and investors who need renovation and maintenance
work done right, Fishbeck Innovations delivers transparent estimating and
dependable construction management — combining hands-on trade experience with
AI-powered cost clarity, so clients always know the scope, the cost, and the plan.

## Core value propositions

1. **Transparent pricing** — clear cost ranges up front, no guessing games.
2. **Investor-minded** — we think in scope, budget, timeline, and ROI.
3. **Local expertise** — Twin Cities codes, suppliers, and labor.
4. **Full-service** — from assessment and estimate to demo, build, and turnover.

## Key messages by audience

- **Homeowners:** "Know what your project costs before you commit."
- **Investors:** "Scope and price your rehab fast, so you can move on the deal."
- **Property managers:** "Reliable rental turns and repairs that keep units rented."
- **Commercial:** "Project management and trades you can count on."
