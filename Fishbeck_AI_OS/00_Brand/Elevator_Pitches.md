# Elevator Pitches

Ready-to-use pitches at different lengths. Pull from these for intros, bios, and openers.

## 5-second
"We're a Twin Cities construction company that gives you a clear estimate and gets
the work done right."

## 15-second
"Fishbeck Innovations handles renovations, repairs, and property maintenance across
the Twin Cities. We give homeowners and investors fast, transparent estimates — so
you always know the scope and the cost before the work starts."

## 30-second
"Fishbeck Innovations is a Twin Cities construction and property maintenance company.
We work with homeowners, real estate investors, and property managers on renovations,
rental turns, assessments, and repairs. What sets us apart is transparency — we use
AI-powered estimating to give you a clear cost breakdown up front, then manage the
project start to finish so it gets done on time and on budget."

## Investor-specific
"If you're buying Twin Cities properties, we'll scope and price your rehab fast so
you can underwrite the deal with confidence — then we manage the turn from demo to
done."

## Networking opener
"I run a construction and property maintenance company in the Twin Cities — we're
the crew that actually gives you a straight estimate before we start."
