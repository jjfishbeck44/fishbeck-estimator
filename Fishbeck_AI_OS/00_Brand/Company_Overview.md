## What we do

Fishbeck Innovations is a construction and property maintenance company serving
homeowners, real estate investors, property managers, and commercial clients
across the Twin Cities. We handle renovations, property assessments, construction
management, estimating, material procurement, investor rehab consulting, rental
turnovers, and demolition/repairs.

## Who we serve

1. **Homeowners** — renovations, repairs, and maintenance.
2. **Real estate investors** — rehab scoping, estimating, and turn management.
3. **Property managers** — rental turnovers, repairs, and ongoing maintenance.
4. **Commercial clients** — buildouts, repairs, and project management.
