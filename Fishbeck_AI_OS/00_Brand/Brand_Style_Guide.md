# Brand Style Guide

How Fishbeck Innovations looks and sounds. Apply this to all content.

## Voice & tone

- **Confident, not boastful.** We know the work; we don't oversell.
- **Plain-spoken.** Talk like a trustworthy contractor, not a corporate brochure.
- **Transparent.** Lead with clarity on scope, cost, and process.
- **Helpful.** Educate the client; respect their budget and time.

Avoid: jargon, hype, vague promises, fear-based selling.

## Writing rules

- Short sentences. Active voice. Second person ("you").
- Lead with the benefit, then the detail.
- Use real numbers and ranges from the Knowledge Base — never invent figures.
- Every page should have a clear call to action (estimate or contact).

## Visual identity

- **Primary color (navy):** `#1B3A5C`
- **Accent color (gold):** `#C8963E`
- **Logo:** "FI" mark, navy + gold (see estimator favicon assets).
- **Look:** clean, professional, trustworthy, with construction warmth.

> Add fonts, logo files, and photo guidelines as they are finalized.

## Naming

- Company: **Fishbeck Innovations LLC** (first use), **Fishbeck Innovations** or
  **Fishbeck** thereafter.
- Product: **Fishbeck Estimator** for the AI cost tool.
