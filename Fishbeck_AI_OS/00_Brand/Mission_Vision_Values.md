# Mission, Vision & Values

## Mission

To make construction and property maintenance clear, fair, and dependable for
Twin Cities property owners — giving every client transparent pricing and quality
work they can trust.

## Vision

To be the most trusted construction and property maintenance partner in the Twin
Cities, and to build software that brings the same transparency to investors and
property owners everywhere.

## Core values

1. **Transparency** — clear scopes, honest pricing, no surprises.
2. **Craftsmanship** — work done right the first time.
3. **Reliability** — show up, communicate, follow through.
4. **Client ROI** — respect every client's budget and timeline.
5. **Innovation** — use technology (AI estimating, future SaaS) to serve clients better.
