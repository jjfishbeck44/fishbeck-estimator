# Service Descriptions

Master descriptions for each service. The website service pages in
`01_Website/Services/` expand on these. Keep both consistent.

## Renovations
Full interior and exterior renovations for homeowners and investors — kitchens,
bathrooms, basements, flooring, paint, and whole-home refreshes. Scoped, priced,
and managed start to finish.

## Property Assessments
Walk-through assessments that document a property's condition, flag issues, and
outline recommended work with cost ranges. Ideal before a purchase, a rehab, or a
maintenance plan.

## Construction Management
We manage the trades, schedule, budget, and quality control so the project gets
done on time and on budget — acting as the single point of contact for the client.

## Estimating
Fast, transparent cost estimates for construction and rehab projects. Powered by
the Fishbeck Estimator and backed by real Twin Cities pricing data.

## Material Procurement
Sourcing and purchasing materials at the right price and quality, coordinating
delivery to keep projects moving.

## Investor Rehab Consulting
Scope-and-cost guidance for real estate investors — turning a property into a
clear rehab plan with budget and timeline, so investors can underwrite deals with
confidence.

## Rental Turnovers
Fast, reliable turns between tenants — cleaning, repairs, paint, flooring, and
make-ready work that gets units rent-ready quickly.

## Demolition & Repairs
Selective demolition and targeted repairs — from tear-out for a remodel to fixing
damage and deferred maintenance.

> Each service should map to a page in `01_Website/Services/`. When adding or
> renaming a service, update both this file and the website folder.
