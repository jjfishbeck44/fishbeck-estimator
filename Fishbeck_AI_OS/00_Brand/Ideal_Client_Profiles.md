# Ideal Client Profiles

Detailed personas. Content should speak directly to one of these audiences.

## 1. The Homeowner
- **Who:** Twin Cities homeowner planning a renovation or facing a repair.
- **Goals:** Quality work, fair price, no surprises, a contractor they can trust.
- **Pain points:** Hard to get clear estimates; worried about being overcharged;
  unreliable contractors.
- **What they want to hear:** "Know what it costs before you commit. We show up
  and do it right."

## 2. The Real Estate Investor
- **Who:** Buy-and-hold or flip investor working Twin Cities properties.
- **Goals:** Accurate rehab scope and budget fast, to underwrite and move on deals.
- **Pain points:** Slow or unreliable estimates; cost overruns; finding a crew
  that thinks like an investor.
- **What they want to hear:** "Scope and price your rehab fast. We think in ROI."

## 3. The Property Manager
- **Who:** Manages rental units or portfolios in the metro.
- **Goals:** Fast, dependable turns and repairs to minimize vacancy.
- **Pain points:** Slow turns, unreliable vendors, inconsistent quality.
- **What they want to hear:** "Reliable turns and repairs that keep units rented."

## 4. The Commercial Client
- **Who:** Small business or commercial property owner needing buildout or repairs.
- **Goals:** A managed project, clear communication, dependable trades.
- **Pain points:** Coordination headaches, budget uncertainty, downtime.
- **What they want to hear:** "One point of contact. On time, on budget."
