# Internal Linking Strategy

Internal links spread authority and guide users to conversion. Apply these rules
whenever creating or editing pages.

## Rules
1. **Homepage** links to all core service pages and main service areas.
2. **Service pages** link to relevant service-area pages and the estimator/contact.
3. **Service-area pages** link to all service pages and the homepage.
4. **Blog posts** link to related service pages and 1–2 related posts.
5. **Case studies** link to the relevant service page and a CTA.
6. Every page links to a **conversion** point (estimate or contact).

## Anchor text
Use descriptive, keyword-relevant anchors (e.g., "Twin Cities rental turnovers"),
not "click here."

## Hub-and-spoke
- Hub: `01_Website/Service_Areas/Twin_Cities.md`
- Spokes: city pages + service pages

> Update links when pages are added or renamed to avoid orphans and broken links.
