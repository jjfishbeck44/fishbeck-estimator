# Subcontractor Directory

Vetted trade partners with Minnesota license verification, insurance tracking, and
performance grading. This is operational data — keep it accurate and current. Do not
share sensitive partner details in public content.

> Structured roster lives in `Subcontractor_Directory.csv` (same folder). This file
> holds the process and grading rubric.

## Vetting checklist (before adding a partner)
- **MN license verified:** check at the MN Dept. of Labor & Industry (DLI) license
  lookup; record license number, type, and status.
- **Insurance verified:** Certificate of Insurance (COI) on file — general
  liability and workers' comp; record carrier and **expiration date**.
- **W-9 on file** for 1099 reporting.
- **References / sample work** reviewed.
- **Trade agreement / rate sheet** on file.

## Insurance expiration tracking
- Track every COI **expiration date** in the CSV.
- Flag any partner within 30 days of expiration; do not schedule expired-coverage
  partners until renewed.

## Performance grading (A–F)
Grade after each job; keep a rolling average.
- **A** — on time, on budget, quality, clean, great communication
- **B** — solid, minor issues
- **C** — acceptable, needs oversight
- **D** — recurring problems, use only as backup
- **F** — do not use

Grade factors: quality, timeliness, budget adherence, communication, safety/cleanup.

## Trades to cover
Electrical, plumbing, HVAC, drywall, paint, flooring, roofing, concrete, framing,
carpentry, demo/haul, cleaning, landscaping/exterior.
